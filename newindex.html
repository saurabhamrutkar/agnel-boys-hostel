Our Facilities

Well Designed Rooms

The hostel rooms are spacious with natural light and ventilation.Each student is provided with a steel cot, a study table and chair and a mattress.

Canteen

The Canteen serves snacks, beverages, breakfast,lunch and dinner with a variety of items which suit the needs of students belonging to different geographical locations of the Country and even abroad. Frequent random checks are carried out to ensure that the food served by the Food Court is hygienic, tastes good and is made from good quality ingredients.

TV Lounge & Games

Common TV lounge with cable connection on the first floor of hostel building.The common area also consists of indoor games such as Carrom, Table Tennis.

Gymnasium & Outdoor Sports

State of the art, sports infrastructure has been created on the campus which includes the following Gymnasium, Football turf, Volleyball court, Swimming pool, Basketball court.

Wifi & Laundry

There is Wi-fi faility for hostel students after college hours and 24/7 on holidays. There are washing machines provided in common area for laundry.

Security

There is 24/7 CCTV surveillance on each floor of hostel ensuring safety. There is also a security incharge for hostel.
